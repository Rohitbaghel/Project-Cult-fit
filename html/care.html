Is therapy for me?
Everyone goes through challenging situations in life, and while you may have successfully navigated through other difficulties you’ve faced, Therapy provides the extra support when you most need it. In fact, therapy is for people who have enough self-awareness to realize they need a helping hand, and that is something to be admired.

What should I expect during my first 1 on 1 therapy session?
The first session is about building trust. Therapy works best when you can trust your Therapist and vice versa. We, therefore, use the first session to understand your problems better and define a goal based journey on what to expect from therapy and by when.

What is the difference between a Therapist and a Psychiatrist?
A Therapists' goal is to help patients make decisions and clarify their feelings in order to solve problems without any medication. A Psychiatrist, on the other hand, helps diagnose mental health issues, and prescribes medicines if needed. However, Therapists often works in tandem with a Psychiatrist to provide comprehensive care as needed by any user.

A Therapy pack helps with continuity of your wellness journey, without having to bother about booking sessions one at a time. Your Therapist will recommend the number of sessions that you might need to address your challenges. We recommend you buy a pack only after a discussion with the therapist.

Online personal Training

More and more people are realizing the importance of staying fit and healthy. With a sedentary lifestyle becoming more of a norm these days, it has become essential to find time in your busy schedule for a physical workout. If you are on a time crunch, it might be difficult to squeeze out enough time to go to a training centre or a gym to get your workout done. This is where online personal training comes in handy. You do not need to waste any time commuting, yet still, get access to online personal training from the comfort of your home. An online personal training program constitutes undergoing training under the guidance of a certified personal trainer over a video call. The trainer will be able to guide you through the different workouts and keep an eye on whether or not you are following perfect form while working out. An Online Fitness Trainer is well versed in coaching clients online. You will be able to make the most of each session and burn a lot of calories as you put your body through a vigorous workout. One concern that most people have about joining an online personal training program is about investing in workout equipment. Fortunately, online fitness coaches are trained to design workout programs based on the workout equipment you have at home. Online training is a flexible and cost-effective way to reach your fitness targets without having to rely on the trainer’s location or availability.

How Online Personal Training Works?

Online personal training is a relatively new concept. Ever since its conception, more people have started switching to this type of training because of the many benefits it offers. Online training can be described as an exciting way to start training under the guidance of a certified personal trainer at a fraction of the cost without having to worry about the logistics or travel time. Most online coaching programs have different packages based on the difficulty level as well as the number of hours you will get with the coach. You can pick a package that meets your requirements. If you are a beginner, it is advisable to start with one of the basic packages and then top up as you get more proficient with the help of the online gym trainer. The packages will also give you an outline of what to expect, any equipment you might need, and so on. The number of sessions included in the package will also be mentioned. Once you choose a package, you can choose from the different time slots available. Log in on the chosen day and time and start working out. You will need to leave your video and microphone on in order to be able to communicate with the trainer during your sessions. The trainer will demonstrate the different workouts and keep a close eye on your posture and form. The trainer will also give you plenty of input on how you are doing. Basically, an online personal training program involves the following steps: Signing up for a Personal Training journey and choosing a pack of your choice. Setting your fitness goal and getting started accordingly. Choosing from the wide variety of available workouts. Following the guidance of the trainers. Achieving your goals with regular assessments and sessions. The initial sessions of most online personal training programs are less intense to help you ease your body into workout mode. As you attend more online sessions, you will notice that your body has built up stamina and can handle intense workout sessions with more ease. Since these Personal Training sessions are one on one, you can work out at your pace and benefit from the vast knowledge of the Online Personal Trainer you have chosen.

what are the Benefits of Online Personal training

If you are still sceptical about hiring an online fitness coach, take a close look at some of the benefits offered by online training: Convenience: Since you do not need to reach any location to go ahead with your workout, online training sessions offer a lot more convenience when it comes to managing your schedule. Affordable: One-on-one sessions by gym trainers in person are often expensive. If you are not able to afford in-person training, online personal training can be a godsend. The sessions are a lot more affordable and yet offer the complete benefit of an in-person training session. Communication: Online Personal Trainers are more open to communication throughout the day. You can drop a text, call, or video call. Online personal trainers often check up on their clients routinely to encourage them to stick to a healthy diet and continue working out. No Geographical Boundaries: Online personal training sessions open up the opportunity for you to train under any of the top personal training experts in the country. You are no longer bound by geographical proximities when it comes to looking for an Online Personal Trainer. Motivation & Accountability: With your trainer just a click away, you will be motivated to stay on track and keep up with your workout routine and diet regime. You will also feel more accountable for your actions, helping you reach your fitness goals sooner rather than later. Tracking Results: Online Fitness Trainers will often track the different variables related to your body weight. This will give them a better idea of how to customize the program to help you achieve your target that does not harm your health.
